About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio!

I’m Kyle Dumalaog, an Information Technology student with a strong interest in multimedia, web design, and 3D modeling. I’ve learned the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, UI/UX design, and Blender through my studies, and I’m currently working on building real-world experience and improving my skills further. What excites me about multimedia is the creativity it allows — from designing clean, user-friendly interfaces to exploring 3D environments in Blender. I’m still growing, but I’m proud of the progress I’ve made so far and eager to keep learning.

Beyond tech, I’m also passionate about volleyball. It helps me stay focused, balanced, and driven — traits I bring into everything I do, whether it's on the court or behind the screen.

This portfolio is a snapshot of my journey as I continue to explore and develop as a creator. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you as I keep pushing my boundaries and reaching for the next level.
