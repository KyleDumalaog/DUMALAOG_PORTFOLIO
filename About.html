About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio!

I’m Kyle Dumalaog, an Information Technology student with a growing interest in multimedia, web design, and 3D modeling. I’m currently learning the basics of HTML, CSS, UI/UX design, and Blender, and I’m excited to continue improving and expanding my skills. What draws me to multimedia is how it blends creativity and technology. Even though I’m just starting out, I enjoy experimenting and discovering what’s possible with each new tool I learn.

Outside of tech, I’m also passionate about volleyball. It keeps me active, focused, and reminds me of the value of discipline and teamwork—qualities that I try to carry into my studies and projects. My journey is just beginning, and I’m motivated by the desire to keep learning, growing, and exploring new challenges. I may only have the fundamentals right now, but I’m committed to putting in the time and effort it takes to level up.

Thanks for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to sharing my progress and connecting with others who are also on the path of creativity and self-improvement!
